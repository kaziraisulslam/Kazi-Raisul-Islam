# Article 10: Data Governance & Bias Mitigation
## Objective
Ensures training data is representative, free of errors, and traceable to prevent systemic bias in high-risk AI models.

## Engineering Enforcement
- **Lineage:** Cryptographic SHA-256 hashing verifies the exact data version used.
- **Bias Monitoring:** Automated scan of demographic variables to ensure <5% variance.

## Audit Artifact
The `artifact.json` generated by this control provides the "Paper Trail" required by EU regulators.
