# Industry 4.0 Lean Factory Optimizer & Sustainability Dashboard

## 📊 Executive Summary & Business Case
In modern industrial manufacturing operations (Automotive, Semiconductors, Electronics), profitability is heavily dictated by resource utilization. Unmonitored material scrap, peak-load energy consumption, and unexpected machine downtime directly compress corporate net margins. 

This project delivers an automated **Operational Audit Engine** designed to ingest raw factory floor machine data, calculate key performance indicators (KPIs), identify process waste, and track compliance against green sustainability metrics.

### 📈 Strategic Business Value Metrics
* **Waste Mitigation:** Identifies high-scrap shift operations to reduce material waste by a target 12%.
* **Energy Cost Optimization:** Pinpoints high kilowatt-hour (kWh) surges per unit produced to assist floor managers in shifting high-load processes to off-peak utility pricing.
* **Executive Decision Readiness:** Compresses thousands of daily machine sensor lines into a 1-page financial impact dashboard for operations directors.

## 🎛️ Operational KPIs Tracked
1. **OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness):** Measuring availability, performance, and quality rates on the assembly line.
2. **Scrap Rate Percentage ($SR\%$):** Tracking material variance to lower cost of goods sold (COGS).
3. **Specific Energy Consumption (SEC):** Evaluating energy efficiency by mapping total energy used against overall production output ($kWh / Unit$).

## 🛡️ Industrial Governance & Security Baseline
To protect production integrity within an Industry 4.0 environment, this system architecture accounts for data logging boundaries defined by the **SEMI E187 Cybersecurity Standard**, ensuring secure parameter inputs from edge devices to enterprise reporting networks.
